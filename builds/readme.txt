NOTE on released binary files:
- SRAM builds (have NO suffix or '_sram' suffix) - compiled with 'no_flash' option, no flashing needed, just drag & drop the image into the PICO emulated disk
- FLASH builds (have '_flash' suffix) - are targeted for those who want to flash the interpreter to the PICO (those builds are not tested now)

NOTE on support status:
- v1.0 is not supported any longer
- v1.1 is currently supported version
